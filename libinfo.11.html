AVS - Gaskill Hall Media Call Number Meanings 
Media call numbers have letters and numbers (i.e., F-67)
     Letter-number"v" indicates a videotape (3/4" or VHS)
     Letter-number indicates a 16mm film
     "sl/t" indicates a slide tape set
     "sl" indicates slides
     "FS" indicates videocassette materials shelved as Film Studies
