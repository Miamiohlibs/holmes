WHAT IS THE MAP COLLECTION?
  Brill Science Library owns more than 70,000 maps produced by various 
  governmental agencies and commercial establishments.  Maps produced by
  the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) and Defense Mapping Agency (DMA) 
  constitute the largest part of the collection and are received as
  government depository material.  Also Available are cartographic 
  reference tools, indexes to map series, various atlases and aids in
  the use of maps.

MAP USE POLICIES
  - The collection is open to the public.
  - Maps are for IN-LIBRARY use only.  They may be photocopied subject
    to Fair Use Guidelines.
