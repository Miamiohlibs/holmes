What's in Sherlock

Sherlock includes records for most of the Libraries' book, periodical, sound recording and other media holdings. Collections not well represented include:

Government Documents
Most documents published and received before 1976 are not yet included in Sherlock. If you think a desired item may be a government publication and you have not found it in Sherlock, inquire at the Documents Help Desk, 3rd floor King Library.

Special Collections
Records for many of the items located in Special Collections are not yet in Sherlock. Inquire at Special Collections, 3rd floor King Library for additional information about materials available.

Microforms
The Libraries have several million items available in microform reproduction. Many of these microforms are not yet represented in Sherlock. For assistance determining if a desired item is available in microform, inquire at the King Library Help Desk.

Maps
Most of the Libraries' maps are not yet included in Sherlock. Inquire at the Map Room, Brill Science Library, Hughes Hall for assistance with maps.

External Collections
Sherlock includes brief records for many of the video tapes disseminated by MCIS, Applied Technologies. Records are now being added for the book and periodical collection in the CPPO. Sherlock records for materials in other collections external to the Libraries are planned.
