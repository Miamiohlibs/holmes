Author/Title Search

  1. Type the AUTHOR's LAST NAME first in the AUTHOR box:

    AUTHOR

    for example:

    • Shakespeare
    • Shakespeare William
    • Bach, J

    You can also type in the name of an artist, composer, government body, organization, or corporation.

    for example:

    • United States Congress
  2. Type as many of the words in the title as you know in the TITLE box:

    TITLE

    for example:

    • The merchant of Venice
    • Venice
    • Sonata no. 6
