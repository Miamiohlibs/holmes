Storage Retrieval 

BRILL SCIENCE LIBRARY
    For items published before 1960, complete the storage retrieval
form which you obtain from the Reference or Circulation Desk.

    Give the completed form to the librarian or circulation staff.

    Items are retrieved on demand.

KING LIBRARY

    Check items with Dewey Call numbers (begins with a number), at
Reference Desk to see if it is in storage.  Complete the storage
retrieval form which you obtain from the Circulation Desk.
